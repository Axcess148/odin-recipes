Lemon Chicken Thighs

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 3 russet potatoes
  • 1 TBL kosher salt
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 rounded TBL dried oregano
  • pinch of cayenne
  • 6 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • fresh chopped oregano to garnish

Steps

  1. Add chicken thighs to a large bowl
  2. Toss with salt, rosemary, oregano, cayenne, garlic, lemon juice and olive oil
  3. Peel and quarter the russet potatoes and add to the bowl to toss with the rest of the ingredients (you could cook this right away or let the chicken marinade for a few hours beforehand)
  4. Transfer everything into a large roasting pan with the chicken's skin side up, saving any juices left in the bowl
  5. Add about 1/2 cup chicken broth to the pan to keep things from sticking to the bottom
  6. pour the leftover juices of the marinade over the chicken thighs
  7. Transfer the pan in the center of a 425 degree oven for 45 minutes, tossing every 15 minutes or so
  8. When finished, remove the pan from the oven and transfer the chicken to a serving platter
  9. Set the oven to broil and add the potatoes back into the oven on the top rack
  10. broil the potatoes until a nice golden crust forms, about 3 to 5 minutes
  11. add the potatoes to the platter and pour the remainder of the chicken broth to the bottom of the pan to loosen up any bits remaining
  12. Strain the sauce through a seive and pour over the chicken and potatoes
  13. Garnish with the fresh chopped oregano and your finished, Enjoy!
